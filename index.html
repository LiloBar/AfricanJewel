BE THE CHANGE THAT YOU WISH TO SEE IN THE WORLD

African Jewel Volunteering Foundation is dedicated to providing care, attention, and smiles to disadvantaged and ill children in the townships of Cape Town. We offer educational and fun activities, supervise meals and homework, and ensure a safe environment for children to thrive.

Find Out More

We've got what you need!

African Jewel Volunteering Foundation recruits and hosts volunteers world-wide and invites them to share their time and expertise by assisting disadvantaged children, their less fortune families and communities by assisting teachers, educators and care givers with their tasks and responsibilities.

With our organisation, international volunteers and our local children will be able to learn about other cultures and life skills like urban farming, reading and other skills in a safe environment in the beautiful Gauteng region of South Africa.
